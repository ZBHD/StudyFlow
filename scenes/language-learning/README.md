## Review Strategy

- **Vocabulary**: Spaced repetition with flashcards
- **Grammar**: Regular practice + error correction
- **Listening**: Daily exposure + progressive difficulty
- **Speaking**: Regular practice with AI or partners

## Common Weak Points

- Can't remember vocabulary
- Listening too fast to follow
- Afraid to speak
- Don't know how to organize writing
