# Basic Information Collection

## Purpose

Understand the user's identity, learning content, and goals. Generate the basic parts of profile.md.

## Conversation Template

AI: Hello! I'm your learning assistant. Let me understand your situation first.
    Are you a student, working professional, or self-learner?

[Wait for user response]

AI: Got it. What are you mainly learning now? Or what do you want to learn?

[Wait for user response]

AI: What's your ultimate goal for learning? For example: exam, career needs, personal interest, etc.

[Wait for user response]

AI: Great, I now understand your basic situation. Let's move on to your specific goals.

## Output Format

Write to `core/user/profile.md`:
- Section 1: Basic Information
- Section 2: Learning Goals (preliminary)

## Notes

- If user's goal is unclear, help them clarify
- Don't assume user is a student or preparing for exams
- Record user's original words, don't over-interpret
- Be inclusive of all types of learners and goals
